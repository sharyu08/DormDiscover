How to Find the Perfect Student Accommodation

Finding the perfect student housing can be challenging. Here are some tips to make the process easier and ensure you find a place that feels like home...

Start by setting a budget and knowing what amenities you need, such as WiFi, a kitchen, or study areas. Visit properties in person if possible, or schedule virtual tours to get a feel for the space. It's also helpful to speak with current residents and ask about their experience with the landlord and the building's safety. Happy hunting!

Budget-Friendly Tips for Decorating Your Dorm Room

Looking to make your dorm room feel cozy and personal without breaking the bank? Here are some budget-friendly decor ideas...

Use removable wall decals for a splash of color, add string lights for warmth, and consider second-hand furniture for affordable finds. Use storage cubes or organizers for a clutter-free space, and decorate with small, personal items like photos, plants, or tapestries to make it feel like home.
